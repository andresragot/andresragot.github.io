ABOUT ME

One of my very first memories from my childhood was when my big cousin handed me over his 64 controller because he was playing Ocarina of Time, and I was by his side. Since that exact moment, video games in general have been over my head all the time. Even if I am not thinking about them, I am thinking about the second best thing I love, and that is doing them. Or more specifically, how to do them.

All the curiosity turned me into the professional I am today, which is very eager to learn a lot about video games, and will never not want to keep learning.

In 2017, I embarked on a life experience, leaving my country with 17 years and studying in INSA de Lyon. However, in 2021, I started what will become my main focus at UDIT, studying a bachelor’s degree in video game design and development.

I have learnt many things along the way, gathered new insights and different perspectives. I have also mastered new languages, new engines, new tools, met new people, improved my coding abilities. In my spare time, I work on personal projects which either way I am always learning.

My passion for video games has driven me to pursue a career in this field. I am always eager to learn more and improve my skills. I hope to create innovative and engaging games that will entertain and inspire others.
